Objective

The first player to reach 10,000 points starts the end game. After the end game starts, the rest of the players get one last turn. The player with the most points at the end of the game wins.

Rules

  • Players take turns rolling six dice.
  • On each turn, the player rolls and chooses any dice that can be scored (holding).
  • The player can hold any number of dice, but must hold at least one die.
  • The player can then keep the dice (locking).
  • The player then either rolls the remaining dice or stops and scores the points (banking).
  • The first time a player banks points, it must equal 500 or more.
  • If the player rolls and cannot score any points, they lose all points for that turn (bust).
